Alastair's research is centred around imprvoing the performance and sustainabilit of battery applications, from the commercially available technology. This includes the development of cell-level and pack-level modelling methods, enhancing understanding in the relationship between temperature and performance, understanding operational degradation and pack-level diagnostics to improve second-life technology, and examining the environmental and societal benefits of sodium-ion battery tecnhnology. Alastair's research background is in experimental design, heat transfer and computational fluid dynamics.
